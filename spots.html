Malaysia's First

Miri Velosolutions
Pump Track

A historic milestone for Malaysia's extreme sports scene! Located at Taman Bulatan Miri, this facility is a 251-metre long asphalt track built to elite international UCI standards.

It offers flowy waves, deep berms, jumps, and rollers designed to challenge professional athletes while remaining accessible for young beginners on BMX, dirt jumpers, and skateboards.

Local Hotspot

Miri Skatepark

Featuring massive, stunning street graffiti visible from above, the Miri Skatepark is the heart of the local freestyle scene. Packed with ledges, ramps, banks, and flat ground sections, it's the perfect training ground for BMX riders and skaters to dial in their street and park tricks.
