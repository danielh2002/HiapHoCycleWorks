THE EVOLUTION OF HIAP HO CYCLE WORKS

"More Choice For You." — Pedaling Forward Since 1987

1987

Established

1987

From a Wooden House to the Open Road

Our roots trace back to the quiet river town of Marudi. We began in a small, traditional wooden house with a showroom floor that held only a handful of bicycles. In those early days, we built our foundation on honest, hands-on craftsmanship, treating every bike with meticulous care.

As demand grew, we made the pivotal move to Miri. To meet the rapidly changing needs of a growing city, our business evolved. We expanded our expertise beyond bicycles, transitioning successfully into the tyre industry and, for a time, fully operating in the motorcycle sector.

Before (Pre-2022)

Evolution

A Return to Our True Passion

Despite our success in motorized transport, we saw that our true potential always lay in bicycles. Driven by a love for the sport and a desire to serve the community, we made the bold decision to pivot back to our roots, transitioning into a 100% dedicated bicycle business.

We took the high-level mechanical precision we learned from motorcycles and applied it to the rapidly advancing world of cycling technology, expertly adapting modern engineering to bicycles. Today, we provide that exact same precision service for both professional, high-performance machines and normal, everyday rides.

Uncompromising Precision

2022

The 2022 Transformation

As our vision grew, so did our need for a space that reflected our high standards. In 2022, we underwent a massive shop renovation to completely modernize Hiap Ho Cycle Works. We transformed our traditional storefront into a sleek, professional, and contemporary cycling hub.

This major upgrade was about more than just aesthetics. It allowed us to build a state-of-the-art workspace for our mechanics and a premium showroom to properly display top-tier bicycles and gear, elevating the entire experience for our community.

After (Post-2022)

Present

"More Choice For You"

For over three decades, our guiding philosophy has been simple: "More choice for you." This long-standing motto is the driving force behind our growth and success. By constantly seeking out the best brands and adapting to modern cycling innovations, we ensure that our inventory caters to every type of rider.

From reliable daily commuters and custom builds to elite, high-performance setups, this commitment to offering unparalleled variety has transformed us into a regional powerhouse. Our reputation now extends far beyond Miri. By consistently delivering top-tier service and an unmatched selection of bicycles and gear, we have proudly become the ultimate destination for cyclists across the region—dominating the market and drawing in riders from Brunei, down to Bintulu, and across to Sibu.
